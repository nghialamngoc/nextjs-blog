<H2 id="automatic-static-optimization"> 1. Automatic Static Optimization </H2>

Next.js automatically determines that a page is static (can be prerendered) if it has no blocking data
requirements. This determination is made by the absence of **getServerSideProps** and
**getInitialProps** in the page.

> next/link docs: [https://nextjs.org/docs/advanced-features/automatic-static-optimization](https://nextjs.org/docs/advanced-features/automatic-static-optimization).
